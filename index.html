CIRCUL8

The Circul8 is intended to be an easy to use sequential compression system, prescribed by a physician, for use in the home or clinical setting to help prevent the onset of DVT in patients by stimulating blood flow in the extremities (simulating muscle contractions). This device can be used to: Aid in the prevention of DVT as well as aid in the treatment and healing of: stasis dermatitis, venous stasis ulcers, arterial and diabetic leg ulcers, chronic venous insufficiency and reduction of edema in the lower limbs. Features include Bluetooth connectivity for use with app, battery life meter and easy to read timer and pressure displays. *Uses Round Port Ac Wall adapter charging cable SIZING: Universal.
